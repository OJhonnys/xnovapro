About Xnova Productions

Xnova Productions Corp is a premier, family-owned entertainment company based in Miami. Founded with a deep-rooted passion for creating unforgettable live experiences, the company brings years of dedication, structural engineering, and nightlife expertise to large-scale events.

While the Xnova brand itself is a fresh force, our core team holds a formidable background in the industry. We are the architects behind some of Miami's most iconic gatherings, including three consecutive editions of Deseo Weekend (2022, 2023, and 2024), the high-energy Dream Summer Festival, the sensational Numanice Miami, and most recently, the monumental Super Nova New Year’s Eve 2026.

Our mission goes beyond the party — we project you into a new dimension. Through innovative production, immersive atmospheres, and world-class artist curated lineups, Xnova is more than a production company; it is a gateway to experiences that connect, move, and inspire.

Welcome to the future of events. Welcome to the Xnova galaxy.

— Stage Design & Construction

We design, plan and build every stage.

At Xnova Pro, we bring ideas to life through immersive stage design, custom fabrication, and full-scale scenic production. From concept to final build, our team creates unique environments for concerts, festivals, corporate events, and live experiences. We specialize in 3D stage design, transforming creative visions into detailed visual concepts that combine innovation, functionality, and impact. Our production and construction team then turns those concepts into reality with high-quality scenic fabrication, custom structures, branded installations, and one-of-a-kind stage elements. Whether it’s a large-scale festival stage, immersive event setup, or custom scenic piece, Xnova Pro delivers creative solutions designed to elevate every experience.

— Scenic Production & Environments

We craft immersive scenography & custom builds.

At Xnova Pro, we transform spaces into breathtaking realities through custom scenic design, thematic environments, and high-impact event decorations. Our team specializes in structural storytelling, combining artistry with precise engineering to deliver one-of-a-kind installations for festivals, corporate galas, branded activations, and temporary luxury spaces. From materials selection and architectural drafting to onsite installation, we ensure every texture, lighting integration, and architectural element works in perfect harmony. We construct immersive worlds that captivate audiences, ensuring your event’s visual landscape leaves a permanent mark on every attendee.

— Expertise & Solutions

Comprehensive production services tailored for entertainment.

We combine technical precision with creative vision to deliver end-to-end solutions for live entertainment. Our team takes full accountability for the complete organization and execution of your event, managing everything from initial concepts and licensing to onsite logistics, sub-contractor coordination, and final breakdown. Whether providing high-end LED setups, specialized stage engineering, or full festival infrastructure, Xnova Pro handles the entire project roadmap so you can deliver a flawless, high-impact experience that meets international standards.

3D Stage Design

Conceptual architectural layouts, immersive 3D drafting, and photorealistic blueprints tailored to translate artistic visions into actionable construction designs.

Scenic Fabrication

On-demand custom engineering, corporate brand installations, premium carpentry, and tailor-made scenic components constructed for massive crowd impact.

Structural Rigging
